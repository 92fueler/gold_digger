Mom's Awesome Spaghetti

My mom would make spaghetti for dinner a lot when I was a kid. It wasn't until I was an adult and had moved out of the house that I realized how comforting and delicious her recipe is! When I visit her now I always ask her to make it for me for dinner.

  • 2 Tbs. olive oil
  • 1 green bell petter, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 lb. ground turkey
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sause
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles

Make the spaghetti sause

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the green bell pepper and onion, and sautee until they are soft.
  3. Add the ground turkey to the pan and pan-fry until it is cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.
  4. Once the turkey is cooked through, pour in the jar of spaghetti sauce. Recude the heat to low, stir to combine.

Make the spaghetti noodles

  1. Heat a large pot of water on the high heat, until boiling.
  2. Once boling, add the box of spaghetti noodles and cook following the packages'directions.
